About Purbanchal Roadways

Your Trusted Logistics Partner Since 1997

Our Story

Established in 1997, Purbanchal Roadways has grown to become one of the leading logistics providers in Northeast India. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and excellence in service.

Our journey began with a small fleet of vehicles serving local businesses in Assam. Today, we operate across the entire Northeast region and beyond, offering comprehensive logistics solutions to clients from various industries.

Our Expertise

  • Over Weight Cargo in Hilly Terrains
  • Industrial Goods Transport for Hydro & Power Grid Projects

Our Mission

At Purbanchal Roadways, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and cost-effective logistics solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients. We are committed to delivering exceptional service while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

We strive to be the preferred logistics partner for businesses operating in challenging terrains, particularly in the Northeast region of India.
